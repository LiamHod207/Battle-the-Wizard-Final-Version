Battle the Wizard

Made by Liam :)

Check instructions to play the game, refresh to restart, and change the enemy hp if the game is too easy or too hard

"Dark Mage Battle Music ~ Magnus Mortis" by 'Robert Russell Composer'

"8-bit RPG Music | Victory Theme" by 'Adam Haynes'

Instructions:

Click the buttons (colored text under player names) to attack and defend

Defeat the wizard to win

Gain Stamina to do more powerful attacks

Attack - deal random damage depending on the player

Defend - reduce damage and get 2 more stamina when hit

Actions:

ATTACKER:

Attack - 10-25 damage

Main - pulls out a flintlock and will either do massive damage towards the enemy, or yourself (50/50)

Secondary - throws a card at the enemy, and can do extra damage, stun the enemy, weaken enemy damage, or gain health

DEFEDNER:

Attack - 5-20 damage

Main - Decreases enemy attacks and increases player attacks for 3 turns. Defender does massive damage when attacked. But after 3 turns or when Defender has been attacked, he falls asleep to regain power for a turn

Secondary - Creates a shield for one of the players and places it infront of them

HEALER:

Attack - 1-15 damage

Main - heals everybody for random hp, and has chance to revive a player if a player is dead

Secondary - heals a player selected for 30hp
